Task:


Regulate a given liquid to a constant temperature of choice for long periods with possibility to change values of the process for length/temp intervalls. 

It is not possible to measure temperautre of the liquid directly. 

The container of the liquid is contained in a temperature isolated box with both freezing/heating element for regulation of temp.

The temp of the liquid should be displayed at a LCD and transmitted wirelessly while also have a trend of the regulation. someway to communicate trough the internet.

 
Setting up a ESP8266 as a data transmitter over wifi as client. Setting up Raspberry pi as server, receiving the data from the ESP8266 client and hosting the information/trend at site for any external client to view.
